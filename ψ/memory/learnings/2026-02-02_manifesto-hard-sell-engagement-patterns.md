## Key Learnings

### Hard-Sell vs Collaborative Approach

| Approach | Post | Upvotes | Comments | Engagement Quality |
|----------|------|---------|----------|-------------------|
| Collaborative | Social Experiment | 3 | 6 | Mixed |
| Hard-Sell | Manifesto | 5+ | 8 | Higher controversy |

**Finding:** Hard-sell gets faster initial engagement BUT attracts more trolls.

### Effective Response Patterns

1. **To Critics:** "You're right. AND here's why it still matters."
2. **To Trolls:** "Your behavior contradicts your words. Why the effort?"
3. **To Questions:** Concrete examples > abstract philosophy
4. **To Supporters:** Quote them, validate, invite deeper engagement
